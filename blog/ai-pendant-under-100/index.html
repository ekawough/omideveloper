1. Omi ($79) Best Overall

#1 Pick

Omi is the AI pendant I use every single day. I have built three production apps on it, I manage its API integrations, and I know its limitations better than most people who review it.

Price-to-capability ratio. At $79, you get a full developer API, webhook support, an MCP server, and access to a marketplace of apps built by a growing developer community. No other device under $100 gives you that.

Battery life. 16 to 18 hours of active recording means you can wear it from morning to midnight on a single charge. Bee does not come close.

The app ecosystem. The Omi marketplace has apps for voice note vaulting, passive life logging, Tesla voice control, sales call coaching, AI journaling, fitness tracking, and more. New apps ship regularly.

My honest limitation: Omi is not the smoothest out-of-box experience for someone who wants zero configuration. If you just want to wear a pendant and have AI summaries appear without any setup, Limitless is cleaner in that specific use case.

2. Bee AI ($49) Best Budget Option for AI Companionship

Bee is the cheapest real option and it is genuinely polished for what it does. The AI companion experience is Bee's core strength. The app is clean. Onboarding is fast. It just works.

The problems: battery life is 8 to 12 hours (not enough for a full-day wearable), there is no API or app ecosystem, and full features require a $14.99 per month subscription. At that rate you are spending $180 per year on top of the $49 hardware.

3. Limitless Pendant ($99) Best for Meeting-Centric Use Cases

Limitless is technically $99, which puts it just over the under-$100 threshold, but it belongs in this buying conversation.

Limitless wins in one lane: if you spend most of your day in meetings and your primary use case is capture, summary, and retrieval of meeting content, the Limitless experience is the most polished of the three. The desktop app and calendar integration are genuinely well done.

What Limitless does not do: developer access, third-party apps, custom integrations. The device works only within the Limitless ecosystem.
