How to set it up (90 seconds)

  1. Install the Omi mobile app on your phone (free).
  2. Open Settings, then Devices.
  3. Tap "Connect a third party wearable."
  4. Select Plaud.
  5. Authorize Omi to read your Plaud recordings.
  6. Open the Omi App Marketplace.
  7. Install one of my apps.

That is it. Your Plaud now runs Omi marketplace apps.
