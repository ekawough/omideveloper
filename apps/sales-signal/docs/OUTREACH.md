existing GHL or HubSpot, and run SalesSignal on every door knock. In
exchange: they give us honest feedback and a one-paragraph testimonial if
it works.

Zero cost to them. Zero risk. We eat the Omi device cost and the
infrastructure.

## Who to email in SoCal

Search Google Maps / Yelp / LinkedIn for:
- "solar sales" + your city
- "door to door solar" + Southern California
- "roofing sales team" + your area
- "HVAC door to door" + your area

Look for companies with 10-50 employees — big enough to have a team,
small enough that the owner answers their email.

LinkedIn: search "solar sales manager" + your metro area. Connect with a
personal note, then send the email above as a follow-up.

## What to send them

1. The 5-min demo video (after you record it)
2. A link to the admin panel (live, with a demo session pre-loaded)
3. The GitHub repo (shows it's real, not vaporware)

## What NOT to say

- Don't say "hackathon project" — say "AI tool I've been building"
- Don't say "it's free because I need testers" — say "free pilot because
  I want to make sure it works perfectly for your workflow before pricing"
- Don't mention Omi by name — say "small clip-on mic" or "wearable mic."
  They don't care about the hardware brand.
- Don't lead with the tech — lead with "zero CRM data entry"

## Timeline

- Week 1: Email 10-15 companies. Follow up on LinkedIn.
- Week 2: Demo call with anyone who responds.
- Week 3: Ship them Omi devices + onboarding doc.
- Week 4+: They're using it. You're collecting data.

Even if only 1 out of 15 says yes, that's your case study.
