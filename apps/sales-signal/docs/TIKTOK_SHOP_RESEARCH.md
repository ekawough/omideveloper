**But video still drives raw reach and virality:**
- Carousels underperform video by ~33% on raw shares
- For discoverability, video wins
- Short problem-solution loops (5-15 second payoff) convert best
- Authentic reactions beat polished production (25% reach drop for

**CRITICAL NEW POLICY (January 19, 2026):**

## 5. Major Policy Changes You Need to Know

### Fees (2026)

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Referral fee (standard) | 6% of order value |
| Referral fee (first 30 days) | 3% |
| Payment processing | 1.02-3.78% |
| Blended effective rate | ~7.02% |
| Smart Promotion fee | 3.5% of GMV (+1% during campaigns) |
| Security deposit | $1,500 per store (required) |
| Bank withdrawal | $0.05/transaction |
| PayPal withdrawal | 0.9% service fee |
| Minimum ad daily budget | $50/day (ad group: $20/day) |

**Total effective seller costs: 50-60% of retail price** when factoring all
fees + affiliates + ads. At 65% product margin, actual profit is 5-15% of
retail. This is tight. Volume matters more than margin.

### Shipping (February 2026 reversal)

TikTok reversed its plan to force all US sellers onto TikTok-controlled
logistics. Independent shipping via UPS, FedEx, and seller-managed carriers
is still allowed. BUT:

- All USPS orders MUST purchase and print labels through TikTok's 4PL system
  (January 6, 2026 change). Externally generated USPS labels may be rejected.
- UPS and FedEx can still use seller-generated labels.
- TikTok may revisit this mandate in the future.

### SPS Changes

- SPS is now the ONLY metric for settlement tier and reserve level
- Evaluation window shortened to 60 days (was 90)
- SPS 3.5+ required for: affiliate marketing, campaigns, short settlements,
  Smart Promotion, custom handling time
- SPS 4.5+ earns Star Shop Badge (visible to buyers)

### Account Rules

- Business entity required (sole proprietors and individuals cannot register
  as cross-border sellers)
- Business must be 60+ days old with valid license lasting 90+ days
- "Change of Mind" returns are now a distinct category; sellers cannot
  unreasonably reject these
- AI-generated product images must accurately represent real product appearance

- Functional foods: 265% YoY growth

- Collagen, vitamin C, stress-relief products trending

**3. Home & Lifestyle Gadgets (rapidly growing)**
- Ninja Cordless Blender: 1,800% order surge
- Homeika Cordless Vacuum: $25M+ GMV
- Organization tools, cleaning solutions: strong because payoff is visual
- Problem-solving gadgets that demo in 15 seconds = ideal TikTok product

**4. Fashion & Accessories**
- Accessories outperform full fashion (fewer returns, no sizing issues)
- Magnetic lashes: mature market, 78% repeat use, dual-pack at $32.99 works
- Jewelry, bodysuits, casual dresses performing well

### Pricing Sweet Spots
- Impulse zone: $10-30 (highest volume)
- Conversion peak: $15-35 (up to 8.2% conversion rate)
- Platform trend: Prices fell 14.08% across 24 of 27 categories in 2024.
  TikTok is deliberately driving volume over margin.

### Oversaturated (avoid or differentiate hard)
- Generic makeup and basic cosmetics
- Cheap phone accessories
- Generic "as seen on TV" gadgets without a viral hook
- Anything where established brands already run affiliates

### Still Open
- Specialized wellness tools (specific outcomes, not general supplements)
- Problem-solving home gadgets that demo in 15 seconds
- Fashion accessories (low return rate)
- Niche beauty devices (professional results at home)
- "Desk reset" / productivity wellness products
