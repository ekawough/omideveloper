Manifesto

The thesis

The next AI moat isn't intelligence. It's personal context .

People won't choose the smartest AI. They'll choose the AI that knows them .

Omi is the most personal AI

What's broken

Today's AI is a vending machine: prompt → answer → forget.

Real progress needs continuity: memory, goals, patterns, follow-through.

Advice doesn't change behavior. Timing + context does.

What Omi is

Omi is your personal AI mentor 

It learns your life from what you say, do, and work on.

It doesn't just answer. It nudges .

It doesn't just remember. It pushes .

It doesn't just help. It keeps you honest .

The belief

Personal context beats raw IQ.

A smaller model with your history beats a bigger model with none.

The killer feature isn't chat. It's proactivity . 

The mission

Make humans 1,000× more productive .

How we win

By collecting ALL data about the user

To achieve it, we commit to privacy and open-source

Use the data to give the maximum personalization

Roadmap

Phase 1 — Desktop software (now)

Start where your life already happens: your computer.

Capture the three signals that matter:

Meetings (what you said)

Files (who you are)

Screen (what you're actually doing)

Give back what people crave:

"Here's what you should do today."

"Here's how you should do it"

"Here is what I noticed"

Success isn't "Omi is smart."

Success is: "Omi knows me." 

Milestone before next phase: 200k software users — this milestone will make us self-sustainable (~$100k MRR) and will prove that the product is useful enough to grow organically 

Phase 2 — Necklace (offline life)

Work isn't your whole life. Conversations are.

Capture the world AI never sees: real life .

Turn talk into outcomes: tasks, follow-ups, coaching.

Milestone: 100k necklaces before moving next. This will prove a PMF and make it self-sustainable. Most likely we will kill the necklace after glasses are launched. 

Phase 3 — Glasses (real-time)

Eyes beat ears for context by 10x

AI sees what you see. Understand what you're doing as it happens.

The jump: from "helpful" to situationally aware .

Milestone: 1M glasses before moving next. This will prove a PMF and make it self-sustainable. Glasses are a great formfactor cuz ~3B people already wear glasses 

Phase 4 — Brain (intent)

The final unlock is intent before action .

Start with silent speech.

Then brain signals non-invasive.

Go invasive only when society is ready.

Milestone: 7 billion devices. Our mission is to make ALL humans 1000x more productive. We will connect all humans into a single network/metaverse. At first, it will be in the glasses but eventually the screen will be ditched 

Why now

Models are commoditizing.

Context is the new moat.

The winner is the AI that sticks .

Why us

We ship hardware + software as one system.

We use open source as a trust wedge.

We're not building "a recorder." We are collecting context to build the most personal AI 

thought to action.
