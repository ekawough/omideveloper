FAQ 

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

How does it work?

omiGPT works with all iPhone and Android phone models. It integrates with your ChatGPT app through an omi mobile app.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

Can it hear only me or everyone?

omi can hear all voices but you can change it to listen to only your voice.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

When I'll receive it?

Now

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

Where the conversations are stored?

Conversations can be stored locally on your phone, or on the cloud. Your data is encrypted and everything can be deleted in one click in omi app

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

What happens when I break or lose the device?

You can still access all your conversations and memories via omi mobile app, until you choose to delete it. 

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

I bought a different version. Do I need to buy this as well?

No. If you purchased omi before, it will include the chatGPT integration. If you own a Dev Kit, check "chatGPT" app - it's already live!

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

Do I need a Subscription? 

You don’t necessarily need a subscription — Omi already includes 1,200 minutes of free transcription every month. However, if you’d like unlimited usage, you can upgrade to Omi Unlimited anytime. You can find more details here 

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">
