Frequently Asked Questions 

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full gp-text-[var(--icon-expand-active-color)]">

What is Omi?

Omi is an AI-powered wearable and software platform that captures, summarizes, and organizes real-world conversations — helping sales professionals stay present while never missing a detail.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

How does Omi work?

Wear your Omi device or use your phone to record conversations. Omi transcribes the audio, extracts key insights, people, and action items, and generates a structured summary — available on desktop, mobile, and via CRM integrations.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

What industries is Omi best suited for?

Omi is ideal for real estate agents, car dealership sales reps, insurance advisors, medical device reps, coaches, and other professionals who rely on high-value, in-person conversations.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

What does the trial include?

We offer a 7-day pilot with 5–10 free devices. You place a refundable deposit ($89/device), which is either refunded in full if devices are returned, or applied toward your contract if you continue.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

How is my data protected?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit , and we never use your recordings or summaries for AI model training without your explicit permission. We are actively working toward SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.

svg]:!gp-h-[var(--height-iconCollapseSize)] [&>svg]:!gp-w-full">

Is Omi compliant with recording and privacy laws?

Yes. The Omi wearable includes visual and audio indicators that signal recording is in progress, supporting two-party consent in applicable jurisdictions. Users are responsible for obtaining consent per local regulations.
