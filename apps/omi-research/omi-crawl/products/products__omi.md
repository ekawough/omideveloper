- Transcribes everything you say and hear
- Auto-generates summaries, tasks, and memories

- Precision recognition (Speech Profiles)
- Online/Offline: Record live or without a connection

- Search across summaries, tasks, and memories

- Sync action items with your favorite apps
- Custom summary templates
- App Marketplace (thousands of tools)

- 9,000+ member community on Discord
