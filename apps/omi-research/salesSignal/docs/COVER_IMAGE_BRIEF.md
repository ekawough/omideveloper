# Cover image brief

Final: 1920×1080 PNG, exported to `assets/cover.png`. Used on lablab.ai
and as the YouTube thumbnail.

## Composition (left → right)

- **Left third — Omi device** on a neutral-colored shirt collar. Close
  crop. Warm soft light.
- **Middle third — waveform** flowing left-to-right, dissolving into
  colored segments representing emotion (green = happy, yellow =
  neutral, red = angry). One obvious red spike mid-waveform with a small
  label "suppressed objection".
- **Right third — two CRM logos** (GoHighLevel + HubSpot) with a green
  checkmark in front of each, suggesting "contact written to both".

## Typography

- Top left: "SalesSignal" — weight 700, color `#4fd1c5` on dark backdrop.
- Bottom left, 60% opacity: "Wearable → CRM in 5 seconds."
- Bottom right, small: "Built on AMD MI300X" with the AMD wordmark.

## Color palette

- Background: `#0b0f17` (deep navy, matches admin panel)
- Accent: `#4fd1c5` (teal)
- Positive emotion: `#4ade80` (green)
- Negative emotion: `#e76f51` (terracotta-red)

## Do NOT include

- Stock images of generic "AI brain" or "neural network" graphics
- People's faces (licensing headaches)
- The word "revolutionary"

## Quickest production path

Figma or Keynote → export 1920×1080 PNG. If using an image model (Midjourney,
SDXL), use the prompt:

> wide cinematic banner, deep navy gradient background #0b0f17, on the left
> a close-up of a small white clip-on wearable microphone, in the middle a
> horizontal audio waveform flowing right, the waveform colored in soft
> segments of green and warm red with one obvious red spike, on the right
> two CRM app icons (generic, abstract) with green checkmarks, the word
> "SalesSignal" in teal #4fd1c5 top-left corner, minimalist, sharp,
> product photography lighting, no text other than the title

Then composite the smaller text overlays in Figma since diffusion models
butcher small typography.
